what is art?

Art is a form of communication, as far as we date back to its origin, painting and all different art forms were a way of storytelling (ex. cave paintings). It is a form of portraying human emotions in ways words can’t. A moment of peace in a rushing world. We’ve all had a lifelong bond with a pen/pencil, the art of scratching it just right to obtain what we admire to write or make is what has evolved humanity better than any species. Today, art is therapeutic, something that keeps the beauty alive. And that’s what Shapes Art Academy aims for.

constellations

“Art demands constant observation.” – Vincent Van Gogh

The way of connecting lines at specific points and making a shape, or an object out of it is what we call Constellations. For as the stars play the right role in forming one, the points of all the lines connecting are just as significant. And then what you lead to is a starry night.

days & nights

“All art is but dirtying the paper delicately.” – John Ruskin

To change a drawing into a picture, takes some of the dark. And darkness is nothing but lack of light, and art is a mixture of the daylight as well as the night skies, the constellations, and the highlights. When brought together just right, you become one of those they call; an artist.

what is drawing?

“The object of art is to give life a shape.” – William Shakespeare

Drawing begins with a mere line; it ends with one. We’ve all known to scratch a line, whether horizontal, vertical, or slanting, on a piece of a paper. To establish a foundation for your art, by connecting several ends to help you find your drawing is all the decent and crucial work of lines. And lines lead to shapes. We live in a universe that is made of shapes, one shape or a combination of shapes, every object hides one within. In drawing, we only construct an object, with shapes that we call proportions. To give that object a soul and life, is the art of sketching.

what is sketching?

“The artist sees what others only catch a glimpse of.” – Leonardo Da Vinci

Art is a composition of light and dark, highlights and shadows, shades. A drawing is just a bare piece of paper, but when put proper layers to it, a drawing becomes lively. To recreate that depth of dark and the surface of light, is to bring a sketch to life. A sketch that speaks, remember art is a form of communicating, it is a storytelling. Shades, is the only aspect that justifies the shape and curves of an object. Shading is an art of changing the foundation shapes into objects. A square becomes a cube, a circle becomes a sphere, a triangle becomes pyramid

about

the artist

Prithviraj Phalke is a 19-year-old, self-taught artist from Nashik, Maharashtra. In an attempt to share his prowess through Shapes. He believes, “Art can save lives in ways we don’t realize, & sketching is poetry; with the correct lines drawn together.” At Shapes Art Academy, he teaches & shares lessons as well as experiences, in ways that can make art easier & more fun. Also, selling his artwork which includes custom-commissioned portraits of you & your loved ones.
